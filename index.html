Sloper Ice & Mixed

An open database for ice and mixed climbing in the Canadian Rockies.

Why this is different.

Ice and mixed climbing data is incredibly disparate, with route descriptions living everywhere from paper napkins to facebook posts. Sloper Ice & Mixed is a soltion to this. Founded on the belief that climbing data shouldn't have gatekeepers, we built this platform so that everyone can contribute to the history of ice and mixed climbing in the rockies.

How it works.

Sloper Ice & Mixed is a temporal database. meaning changes are tracked over time. This is important because it better reflects the nature of ice climbing routes, which can fluxuate wildly from season to season.
